Our Mission

Access to High Quality Education

Our mission is to transform FUND-ME into an efficient and effective provider of financial aid to students from poor and working class families in a sustainable manner that promotes access to, and success in, higher and further education and training, in pursuit of South Africa national and human resource development goals.


Collaboration

Foster a community working practice whereby individuals work together for a common purpose,an environment where different backgrounds and varying levels of experience are allowed to work together, everyone benefits.


End Unemployment

Introduce a generation with access to jobs and job security is ensured.A generation where everyone has access to resources and is able to make ends meet and provide for his or her family on an everyday basis.
